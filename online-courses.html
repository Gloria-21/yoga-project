  • Hatha yoga On-Demand

    Through alternating challenging asanas with relaxation postures such as Savasana, we teach our bodies to easily shift from one state of the nervous system to the other. This results in more inner balance and harmony and improved overall health

  • Vinyasa yoga On-Demand

    After some warm ups, some standing stretches, and balancing this class moves through a short and potent vinyasa sequence and ends with core work and meditation

  • Meditative yoga On-Demand

    Enjoy the calming and stress reducing effect of this restorative yoga series. Designed to help you manage the highs, take the edge off or just end the day in the best possible way.
