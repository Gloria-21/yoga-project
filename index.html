Welcome to Yoga Project!

Yoga Project was set up in 2019 in Cambridge by friends Kate and Anna who had a vision of a community yoga space in their hometown. Their mission was to create a place that was accessible and inclusive, providing a full-bodied experience of what yoga is. At Yoga Project you will find yoga in all of it's 8 limbs, not just the physical practices but also the ethical focus, the service to the community, and the opportunity to go deeper into the meditative practices of yoga.

Yoga does not transform the way we see things, it transforms the person who sees.” — B.K.S. Iyengar
